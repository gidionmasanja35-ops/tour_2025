Ngorongoro Accommodations

The Ngorongoro Conservation Area offers a variety of accommodation alternatives, including luxury, public camping areas, exclusive tented camps, and permanent facilities. These options provide tourists with a unique experience, especially at night in the area.

Available facilities include hotels, lodges, public campsites, and special campsites.

Lodges

NCA has six best lodges, five of which are located on the rim of the Ngorongoro Crater and offer spectacular views of the surrounding landscapes. These lodges provide a peaceful and exciting stay.

Special Campsites

There are 49 special campsites across the NCA. These luxurious tented lodges provide the best locations for breathtaking views and unforgettable overnight experiences.

Public Campsite

The Simba A public campsite is equipped with facilities for more than 200 people per night, located conveniently near the NCA entry gate. It offers a true outdoor adventure for budget travelers.

HOLL TOUR SIGNATURE SAFARI ACTIVITIES

Experience the wild in ways no one else offers. These unique excursions go beyond the traditional game drive, giving you exclusive access to conservation efforts and specialized landscapes.

Game Drive

Set out before sunrise with a research ranger to track collared predators using radio telemetry. This hands-on conservation experience offers real-time insights into wildlife behavior and ecosystem dynamics.

Explore

Maasai Stargazer Boma

Join Maasai elders at twilight to explore ancient star lore. Using powerful telescopes, you'll view the Milky Way while learning how celestial wisdom guides traditional life and seasonal rhythms.

Explore

Walking Activities

Walk through geothermal landscapes with thermal cameras to uncover hidden hot springs and geological wonders. This immersive activity blends science, exploration, and photography in nature’s most dynamic settings.

photographic Activities
